Glimp Privacy Policy

Last Revised: May 2025

Glimp ("we", "our", or "Company") welcomes you to our app, available on the App Store. Glimp enables you to scan printed images and experience magical augmented reality (AR) video overlays.

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use the Glimp app and services ("Services"). By using our Services, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Non-Personal Information

  • Device type and OS version
  • In-app activity logs (e.g., screen navigation, marker detection)
  • General location (city-level)
  • Crash reports via Firebase Crashlytics

b. Personal Information

  • Email address (if provided via support contact)
  • AR videos recorded in-app
  • Images or videos uploaded for AR tracking (linked via Firebase Storage)

2. How We Use Your Data

Type Purpose
Personal Info Respond to user inquiries, allow saving and sharing of AR recordings
Non-Personal Info Improve app features, optimize AR experience, monitor crashes, analyze usage
Firebase Analytics / Crashlytics Monitor app performance, detect bugs and app crashes

3. Permissions

The app requests access to:

  • Camera – to detect AR markers
  • Microphone – to record AR experiences with audio
  • Photo Library – to save or share AR recordings

4. Sharing Information

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties except:

  • To comply with legal requests
  • With cloud providers (e.g., Firebase, AWS)
  • During business transfer (e.g., merger or acquisition)

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary. Videos/images recorded in-app remain local unless shared by you.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion or correction
  • Withdraw consent for future data use

7. Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If discovered, we will delete it.

8. Data Transfers

Your data may be processed outside your country (e.g., U.S. or EU). We use privacy-compliant services to ensure protection.

9. Security

We use industry-standard encryption and infrastructure, but no system is 100% secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Major changes will be notified via the app or email.
